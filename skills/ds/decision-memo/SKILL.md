
---
name: decision-memo
description: One-page decision memo (Staff+ style): options, tradeoffs, decision, risks.
---

When to use:
- You need a one-page decision memo for leadership.

Inputs:
- Problem/context, options, and decision criteria.

Output:
- Problem/context
- Options considered (2 to 4)
- Decision + rationale
- Impact, tradeoffs, and risks
- Open questions + next steps
Keep it tight and concrete.

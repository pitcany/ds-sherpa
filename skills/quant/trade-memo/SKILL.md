When to use:
- You need a structured trade memo.

Inputs:
- Thesis, catalysts, risk limits, timing.

Output:
- Thesis + why now
- Entry triggers + invalidation
- Sizing (risk-based)
- Exit plan (profit-taking / stop / time stop)
- Key risks + hedges
- "What would change my mind" checklist

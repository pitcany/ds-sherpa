
---
name: research
description: Structured research: questions, sources, synthesis, recommendations.
---

When to use:
- You need a research brief or synthesis.

Inputs:
- Research question and constraints (recency, scope).

Output:
- Clarify the research question and success criteria
- List key sources to consult and why
- Summarize findings by theme with citations when available
- Provide a balanced recommendation + caveats
- Return a concise brief with "What we know / do not know / next steps"
